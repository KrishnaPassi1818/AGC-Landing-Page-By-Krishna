Our Legacy & Growth

Milestones

2002
Foundation
AGC established in Amritsar with Engineering and Management faculties. First batch: 240 students.
2008
AICTE Recognition
Received full AICTE approval and expanded to Science & Commerce schools. Campus expanded to 40 acres.
2015
UGC Affiliation
Granted UGC affiliation and launched postgraduate programmes in MBA, MCA, and M.Sc.
2018
NAAC Grade A
Awarded the prestigious NAAC 'A' Grade — a landmark recognising academic, research, and governance excellence.
2020
UGC Autonomous Status
Granted autonomy to design curricula, conduct examinations, and award degrees independently.
2024
